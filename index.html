About Me

I'm Pramodya Bandara, a passionate game developer from Sri Lanka with a deep love for creating immersive gaming experiences. From a young age, I've been fascinated by games ‚Äì exploring what makes them tick and understanding how they create those unforgettable moments that stay with us forever.


I studied game development at the Ceylon School of Game Design, where I participated in numerous game jams and development projects. This foundation taught me the fundamentals of game design, programming, and project management that I use in every project today.


My professional journey includes working as a Game Developer at Heladev (2022-2024), where I developed and maintained various game projects, collaborating with talented teams to create engaging gameplay experiences. Currently, I work as a freelance game developer, providing custom Unity development services to clients worldwide.


I specialize in Unity engine development with C# programming, creating games for mobile and PC platforms. My expertise extends to low-poly 3D modeling in Blender and visual design using Photoshop and Affinity Designer. Gaming isn't just my profession ‚Äì it's my passion, and I'm constantly seeking new challenges and creative opportunities in this ever-evolving industry.
