About Me

I'm Pramodya Bandara, a passionate game developer from Sri Lanka with a deep love for creating immersive gaming experiences. From a young age, I've been fascinated by games – exploring what makes them tick and understanding how they create those unforgettable moments that stay with us forever.


I studied game development at the Ceylon School of Game Design, where I participated in numerous game jams and development projects.


My professional journey includes working as a Game Developer at Heladev, where I developed and maintained various game projects, collaborating with talented teams to create engaging gameplay experiences. Currently, I work as a freelance game developer, providing custom Unity development services to clients worldwide.


I specialize in Unity engine development with C# programming, creating games for mobile and PC platforms. My expertise extends to low-poly 3D modeling in Blender and visual design using Photoshop and Affinity Designer.
